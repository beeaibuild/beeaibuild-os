# Government Decision Pipeline

A standardized pathway for transforming raw input
into structured, AI-ready decisions.

## Stages

1. Input Intake  
2. Validation  
3. Tokenization  
4. Reasoning  
5. Cost/Time/Risk Evaluation  
6. Decision Synthesis  
7. Audit Record Creation  

## Guarantees

- determinism  
- traceability  
- fairness  
- reproducibility  
- structural consistency
